FTMK lecturer Room Inventory System

FTMK lecturer Room Inventory System is a system to aid lecturers in Universiti Teknikal Malaysia Melaka to file a complaint to the technician when their furniture such as desk, or chair is in need of fix. this system is developed using HTML, SQL, PHP, and javascript language.

The lecturer page where lecturer can update their profile or submit a complaint about their inventory

The technician page where technician can update their profile or look at the list of complaint

The administrator page where admin can register and manage user, insert new furniture, look at inventory record, complaint record and report.
